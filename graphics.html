Music Visualizer

Final Project for CS123: Introduction to Computer Graphics

This was my final project for my graphics class at Brown, which I worked on with one other student (Sacha Servan-Schreiber). To ensure smooth realtime reaction to the music, this raytracer runs entirely on the GPU. The blurring effect is a result of DOF (Depth of Field) using stochastic sampling. Using GLSL's reflection/refraction functions along with Beer's law allowed us to make colored glass.
